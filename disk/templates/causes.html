About us

Adhravfoundation is a Non- governmental organisation and been registered and authorised by the government with Form 10 A , Section 12A & Certificate and Section 80G and CSR FORM 1 and been working towards the betterment of the society in all aspects. Adhrav foundation was established to initiate our part and responsibility to the society in the year 2021.

We started Adhrav foundation in 2021, since then, we have worked in all the fields trying to do our part such as feeding the homeless people who are suffering in streets, we even feed the stray dogs and provide provisional dry ration kit and other basic necessities to the underprivileged family protection of the environment and awareness campaigns for women and children, and much more.

CURRENT PROJECT

  • “THE BRIDGE” is the collaborative initiative of Adhrav Foundation.The project was established on october 2021. This project aims to build the moral and educational background of the underprivileged children
  • Our Reach On The Bridge Project After our first step in the Pallavaram and Pammal area, we have expanded our project to other parts of chennai. From January 2022, we are working on locations such as Perambur, Maduravoyal, Ambattur, Ashok Nagar, Korattur, Mogappair, Periyar Nagar, Villivakkam, etc. This is a huge step towards this project and we hope to cover all this area under “The Bridge” Project and provide help and support for the children located here. With this collaboration and volunteer participation, this project is all set to go. Various college clubs have come forward to collaborate with for volunteering and interacting with the children in these areas.
  • We are collaborating with college clubs such as Easwari YRC, ISSM Rotary club and VIT. We are looking forward to expand our reach to further more colleges to take part in the project to build social awareness. You can find our work so far in this project from our social media’s.
