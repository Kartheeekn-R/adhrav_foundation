ABOUT ADHRAV

Adhrav foundation is a Non- governmental organisation and been registered and authorised by the government with Form 10 A , Section 12A Certificate,Section 80G and CSR FORM 1.We Adhrav Foundation been working towards the betterment of the society in all aspects and for the further development of the society. Adhrav foundation was established to initiate our part and responsibility to the society.

We started Adhrav foundation in 2021, since then, we have worked in all the fields trying to do our part such as feeding the homeless people who are suffering in streets, we even feed the stray dogs and provide provisional dry ration kit and other basic necessities to the underprivileged families to protect the environment of the underprivileged society.Further we create awareness campaigns for women and children, and much more

MAN'S BEST FRIEND DRIVE

The dogs in the street often are left unnoticed and unattended too. Through our man’s best friend drive, we feed street dogs on regular basis with a good meal filled with all their favourites. The amount of excitement and love that these voiceless animals show is what pushes this drive for Adhrav foundation. We have impacted around 1,23,000 street dogs in total in and around chennai.

PROVISIONAL DISTRIBUTION

The people who remain homeless and reside in slum areas are often left hungry and do not get proper provisions to feed their family on daily basis. From Adhrav Foundation, we have provided provisions for homeless and poor families on monthly basis during the COVID-19 lockdown period. The provisional kit includes rice packets, sugar and salt packets, cooking oil, and some pulses. We are providing provisional kits to the needed areas still through this drive.

THE BRIDGE PROJECT

After our first step in the Pallavaram and Pammal area, we have expanded our project to other parts of chennai. From January 2022, we are working on locations such as Perambur, Maduravoyal, Ambattur, Ashok Nagar, Korattur, Mogappair, Periyar Nagar, Villivakkam, etc. This is a huge step towards this project and we hope to cover all this area under “The Bridge” Project and provide help and support for the children located here. With this collaboration and volunteer participation, this project is all set to go. Various college clubs have come forward to collaborate with for volunteering and interacting with the children in these areas.

FOOD DISTRIBUTION DRIVE

Our first initiative from Adhrav foundation was the food distribution drive. It was the need of the hour during the lockdown period as the whole world was affected with Covid- 19. We were distributing food packets on daily basis to the people who had lost their homes due the virus, homeless people in the streets, street vendors, corporation workers and also Covid positive patients. Our food distribution drive has been successfully going on for a year now. We have covered almost almost all the places in chennai. We have provided around 8,82,000 food packets to the needy people. And we are going to achieve more through this drive in the future. Reactivating hunger for the needy people is one of the crucial aims of adhrav foundation.

SCHOOL KIT PROJECT

Under this initiative, We hope to provide School kits for the underprivileged children and help with their education needs. With this initiative we get close with the children and provide for their educational needs. The School Kits contains of School Bag, Lunch Bag, Notebooks, Water Bottle, Pen Pencil Kit, Geometry Box, Calculator and various other educational needs will be required by the children for the schooling daily purposes. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential and to be educated and your support will help us make that a reality. And this project is step towards building a better society through the development of the children education.
